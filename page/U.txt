[SECTION] U
[HEADER]Unconfined aquifer
An aquifer containing water that is not under pressure; the water level in a well is the same as the water table outside the well.
[HEADER]Uniformity coefficient (particle size distribution)
As used for determining grading of sands. CU: a numeric quantity which is calculated by dividing the size of the opening which will pass 60% of a sample by the size of the opening which will pass 10% of the sample. (symbolically C60/C10=CU)
[HEADER]Unsaturated flow
Movement of water in a soil that is not filled to capacity with water. 
[HEADER]Unsaturated soil
Soil in which the pore spaces contain water at less than atmospheric pressure, as well as air and other gases.
